Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team.

Widely considered one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo holds the records for the most goals scored in a calendar year and in a single season of La Liga, as well as the record for the most UEFA Champions League goals in a single season.
